Dojo Rules
==========
* Always be on time
* Always take off your shoes
* Keep the dojo tidy
* Never bring live blades on the tatami
* Respect your opponents
* Most Sensei's should be respected
* Don't underestimate your opponents
* Personal hygiene is important; if you hadn't had a bath in days, avoid the dojo until you do
* Remember to pay the monthly fee (be ready to be kicked out of the dojo by a huge sumo wrestler if you don't)
